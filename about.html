About Me




College student from Our Lady of Fatima University.

When I was a kid I used to play in these gigantic woods where I used to spend my time playing in the creek, picking up tadpoles, cutting vines and swinging on them. I really had a good time over those woods and that's one of the things that I miss most about my childhood.


But when I'm not in the woods, I'm playing on this magical device called “Computer” that my parents introduced to me at a very early age. This thing blew my mind at that time, because I could not imagine ever living without it. And this is how I started to develop a strong interest in technology.


I'm just an average college student who's trying his best to improve everyday. I'm not aiming to be the #1 student in our class. I'm just aiming for the fact that maybe competing with myself is a better niche than competing with other students.


When I'm not studying, I enjoy watching movies, playing video games and spending time with my family.
